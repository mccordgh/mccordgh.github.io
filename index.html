Inquisitive, collaborative, inventive, and sanguine in the most stressful of situations, contributing to a strong team fills me with an incredible sense of accomplishment and camaraderie.

Learning JavaScript and Python at Nashville Software School, along with their associated frameworks Angular and Django, has rekindled my enthusiasm for programming, recapturing teenage days of coding apps and games in QuickBasic and VisualBasic.

For the last fifteen years I've had the pleasure of writing and recording songs, and touring with scatter the ashes, Mother/Father, and Future Unlimited. One of the highlights of every tour was building relationships with not only the band members, but the crews, managers, promoters, fans, and industry folks of all types. I look forward to bringing this experience and perspective to every aspect of my career.
